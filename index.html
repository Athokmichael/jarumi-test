Empowering Purpose-Driven Organizations

We help nonprofits and social impact ventures transition from donor dependency to long-term sustainability through embedded business development support

Jarumi Ventures Africa empowers purpose-driven organizations to build systems that scale impact and sustainability. With a focus on sustainable practices, we equip nonprofits with the strategic tools, systems, and donor-readiness support needed to scale their impact, sustain operations, and inspire long-term donor confidence.

Across Africa, thousands of nonprofits are doing critical work but lack the business acumen and operational stability to scale or survive donor fatigue. With over 150,000 registered NGOs across the continent, our intervention shifts organizations from fragile to future-proof, unlocking their potential to generate revenue, create jobs, and scale their missions sustainably.

Our Services

Comprehensive, embedded support to help organizations build operational strength and financial resilience

Strategic Business Development Advisory

We co-create realistic, mission-aligned strategic plans and financial models that guide nonprofits toward long-term sustainability and readiness for funding or partnerships.

Systems Strengthening & Implementation

Jarumi supports nonprofits in designing and implementing practical internal systems, compliance, and operations, so they function like efficient, well-run enterprises.

Embedded Capacity Building & Coaching

Beyond one-off training, we embed with teams to offer sustained mentorship, targeted workshops, and hands-on support, ensuring new systems are adopted and optimized.

Financial & Revenue Model Development

We help organizations develop earned-income streams and alternative fundraising models to diversify revenue and build financial resilience beyond donor funding.

Donor Readiness & Institutional Strengthening

We prepare nonprofits to attract and retain mission-aligned funding through robust governance, transparent reporting, and strategic partnership development.

Our Impact

Transforming organizations across Africa's nonprofit ecosystem with measurable results

Creating Sustainable Change

Jarumi Ventures Africa exists to bridge a critical gap in the development ecosystem, helping nonprofits and social enterprises transition from donor dependency to long-term sustainability. By bringing private-sector precision to purpose-driven work, we empower organizations to strengthen their systems, diversify their revenue, and align their strategy for scale.

Our model is deeply collaborative and embedded, ensuring that the solutions we offer are not only relevant but adopted and sustained. We work with organizations to build resilience against donor fatigue and financial fragility.
