Our Strategic Solutions

We bring private-sector precision to purpose-driven work, helping nonprofits transition from donor dependency to long-term sustainability

Our Core Services

Comprehensive, embedded support to help organizations build operational strength and financial resilience

Strategic Business Development Advisory

We co-create realistic, mission-aligned strategic plans and financial models that guide nonprofits toward long-term sustainability and readiness for funding or partnerships.

  • Mission-aligned strategic planning
  • Financial modeling and projections
  • Partnership and investment readiness
  • Revenue diversification strategies

Systems Strengthening & Implementation

Jarumi supports nonprofits in designing and implementing practical internal systems, compliance, and operations, so they function like efficient, well-run enterprises.

  • Operational systems design
  • Governance and compliance frameworks
  • Financial management systems
  • Monitoring and evaluation frameworks

Embedded Capacity Building & Coaching

Beyond one-off training, we embed with teams to offer sustained mentorship, targeted workshops, and hands-on support, ensuring new systems are adopted and optimized.

  • Leadership and team development
  • Skill-building workshops
  • Hands-on implementation support
  • Continuous coaching and refinement

Financial & Revenue Model Development

We help organizations develop earned-income streams and alternative fundraising models to diversify revenue and build financial resilience beyond donor funding.

  • Revenue diversification strategies
  • Earned-income model development
  • Fundraising strategy optimization
  • Financial sustainability planning

Donor Readiness & Institutional Strengthening

We prepare nonprofits to attract and retain mission-aligned funding through robust governance, transparent reporting, and strategic partnership development.

  • Grant readiness and proposal development
  • Donor relationship management
  • Reporting and accountability systems
  • Impact measurement and communication
